Mosquito Blood Hunt

You are a hungry mosquito that needs blood to survive!

Your blood level constantly decreases over time.

Suck blood from humans to replenish your blood level.

Controls:

  • Arrow Left/Right: Turn left/right
  • Arrow Up/Down: Fly forward/backward
  • W/S: Fly up/down
  • Space: Suck blood (when near a human)

Be careful! Humans will remember you and try to swat you!

Getting hit will cause you to lose a lot of blood!

Flying high won't save you - humans will throw rocks at you!
